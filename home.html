We know that everyone has to go to a medical store and look if the medicine required by them is available or not. This results in wastage of physical strength and time.

To overcome this problem, we have created a web application to search for the nearby stores where the user's required medicine is present or not.

Click on the button below to locate all the stores which are at a radius of 5km from your current location.

After clicking on the button below you will be redirected to another page where you can also enter a medicine name. And after clicking on submit, only those stores with that particular medicine will be listed.
